Community savings: an extra 20% off

We offer 20% off our published service rates for seniors, students, and low-income households. That discount applies to the prices listed on our pricing page—mention eligibility when you book, and have any requested ID ready at your appointment or before your remote session.

Who qualifies

Seniors

Customers age 60 or older. Please bring a valid photo ID that shows your date of birth (driver license, state ID, passport, or similar).

Students

Current students in high school, college, or trade school. A current student ID, class schedule, or enrollment letter is fine.

Low-income households

If paying our standard rates would be a real hardship, you may qualify. We keep this simple—just tell us when you reach out, and we will treat your request respectfully without a pile of paperwork.

How to use it

  1. Check the services and rates on the pricing page.
  2. Email or call and say you are requesting the community savings rate before we schedule work.
  3. For seniors and students, have your ID ready so we can note eligibility in your booking. Low-income eligibility is handled through a short conversation when you contact us.
  4. The 20% is applied to the final service total for qualifying work—we will confirm your discounted total before we start.
